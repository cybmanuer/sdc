BBM full form is Bachelor of Management. It is an undergraduate course comes up with a duration of 3 years.
  • BBM course deals with a strong foundation towards entrepreneur along with the management skills which is required for the growth of any industry.

  • BBM Course curriculum designed to cover most core concepts of business in-depth such as management principles, Economics, Financial Accounting, Cost Accounting etc.

  • Eligibility criteria to take up this course is higher secondary graduates (10+2 or 12 grade) from a recognised board.

  • This BBM course have greater impact on the growth of the business, the job scope is very high.

  • The aim of the Department of Business Management is to create a stimulating and challenging atmosphere for students to hone their management skills in order to become dynamic leaders in the business world.

  • The faculty members are not only well qualified but also have immense practical knowledge about the inner working of a business enterprise, and this makes them proficient trainers rather than just teachers.

  • The Department regularly conducts field trips to equip students with the necessary skills of business management.

  • BBM course provides knowledge about the areas covered in the business including organizational behavior, critical thinking, and analytical skills.The course is delivered through lectures from experienced faculties and guest lectures from experts.
